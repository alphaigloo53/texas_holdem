Here are some fun facts about the hands you can get along with the corresponding probabilities!

Hand Odds:

  1. Royal Flush: 0.00015%
  2. Straight Flush: 0.0014%
  3. Four of a Kind: 0.024%
  4. Full House: 0.144%
  5. Flush: 0.197%
  6. Straight: 0.392%
  7. Pair: ~43%

These probabilities are why betting and bluffing are huge in the game, and its a lot more about strategy than most people think

Poker Vocab

Content:

  1. Blinds: Forced bet to start the action
  2. Button: Marks the dealer (switches every hand)
  3. Pot: Chips being played for
  4. All in: Betting all your chips
  5. Showdown: When players reveal hands
